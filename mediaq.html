Mountaineering, often called alpinism when done in the Alps, is the set of activities that involves ascending mountains. Mountaineering-related activities include traditional outdoor climbing, skiing, and traversing via ferratas. Indoor climbing, sport climbing and bouldering are also considered mountaineering by some. Unlike most sports, mountaineering lacks widely-applied formal rules, regulations, and governance.
